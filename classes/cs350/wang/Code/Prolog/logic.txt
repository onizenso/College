      A Table of Logical Equivalences
=================================================
Notation: '<=>' is logical equivalence;'~' is NOT
'->' is inference; ' ^ ' is AND; 'v' is OR

commutativity
-----------------------------------------
(A ^ B) <=> (B ^ A) 
-----------------------------------------
(A v B) <=> (B v A) 
-----------------------------------------
associativity
------------------------------------------
((A ^ B) ^ C) <=> ((A ^ B) ^ C)
------------------------------------------
((A v B) v C) <=> ((A v B) v C)
------------------------------------------
double negation elimination
------------------------------------------
(~(~A)) <=> A 
------------------------------------------
contraposition
------------------------------------------
(A -> B) <=> ((~ B) -> (~ A)) 
-------------------------------------------
implication elimination
-------------------------------------------
(A -> B) <=> (~ A  v  B) 
--------------------------------------------
biconditional elimination
--------------------------------------------
(A <=> B) <=> ((A -> B)  ^  (B -> A)) 
--------------------------------------------
de Morgan
--------------------------------------------
(~ ( A  ^  B)) <=> (~ A  v  ~ B)
--------------------------------------------
de Morgan
---------------------------------------------
(~ ( A  v  B)) <=> (~ A  ^  ~ B)
---------------------------------------------
distributivity of  ^  over  v 
---------------------------------------------
(A ^ (B v C))<=>((A ^ B) v (A ^ C))
---------------------------------------------
distributivity of  v  over  ^  
---------------------------------------------
(A v B ^ C))<=>((A v B) ^ (A v C)) 
----------------------------------------------
