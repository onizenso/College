.:Design Models:.

Waterfall

'V' model

Spiral Model:
    Four Phases:
        Planning
        Risk Analysis
        Engineering Phase
        Validation

    Cycle through four phases until reaching the final deliverable. 

Prototype: product that meets/exemplifies the requirements set forth by the 
User, but may/should not include any of the full-fledged features of a 
final deliverable. Prototypes are used to refine User needs. 

Prototypes may be: 
    Throw-away - large unknowns are being tested 
    Evolutionary - small unknowns are being tested, core is functional/known
