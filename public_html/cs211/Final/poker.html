Let's Play Poker

Instructions:

  1. Click "Get a Deck" to get your deck of cards.
  2. Click "Shuffle deck" to shuffle your deck. To get a good shuffle, click the shuffle button four times.
  3. Click "Deal 5 Cards" to deal yourself a hand.
  4. Click on whichever cards you would like to exchange. Note: you can only exchange 3 cards.
  5. Click on "start new game" to begin again. Note: you will have to reshuffle the deck.
